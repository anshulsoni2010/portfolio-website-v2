About Myself

Hey There, I'm Anshul Soni! I'm A Young And Talented Front End Developer Who Absolutely Loves Coding. I've Been Working With Websites Since I Was Just 11 Years Old, And I've Been Hooked Ever Since! I'm The Founder Of CodeINBlogs, A Non-Profit Organization That Provides An Easy-To-Use Platform For Bloggers To Publish Their Articles On A Wide Variety Of Topics.

I'm Currently A 7th Grader At Noble International School, And I Have Eight Months Of Professional Experience In Front End Development. I Just Love Building New Things, And My Passion For Coding Is What Led Me To Create CodeINBlogs. I'm Also The Founder Of Another Non-Profit Organization Called TalentBoost, Which Is All About Helping Young People Who Are Passionate About Coding To Enhance Their Skills And Find Opportunities In The Field.

In My Free Time, I Like To Create Content On LinkedIn To Share My Thoughts And Ideas With Other People Who Love Coding As Much As I Do. I'm Always Up For Meeting New People And Collaborating On Projects, So If You're Interested In Working Together, I'd Love To Chat!

I'm Constantly Learning And Growing, And I'm Currently Focused On Gaining A Deeper Understanding Of NodeJS. I Have Experience Working With HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Git, And GitHub, And I'm Interested In All Aspects Of Web Development, Including FrontEnd And BackEnd.
